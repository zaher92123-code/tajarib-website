Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Tajarib ("we", "our", "us") operates the Tajarib mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (chosen by you)
  • Profile avatar (chosen by you)

When you use the app, we may collect:

  • Location data (only when using map features, with your permission)
  • Photos (only when you choose to upload them for bingo challenges, reviews, or community posts)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used) to improve the app experience

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Show you nearby places and calculate distances
  • Verify bingo challenge completions
  • Display your contributions in the community feed
  • Track your exploration progress (XP, levels, badges)
  • Send you relevant notifications (with your permission)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies. Photos are stored in secure cloud storage. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Location Data

Location access is optional and only used when you actively use map features or check distances to places. We do not track your location in the background.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (authentication and database)
  • OpenFreeMap / MapLibre (map display)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of notifications
  • Revoke location permissions through your device settings

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@tajarib.app
