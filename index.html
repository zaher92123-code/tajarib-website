What You Get
Every feature built for explorers
Not another booking app. Tajarib is built for the curious — the ones who chase sunsets down wadis and find forts hidden behind mountains.

Discover Places

416 MHT-verified heritage sites. Forts, souqs, archaeological sites, natural wonders — all with GPS coordinates, photos, and local tips.

Self-Guided Routes

Curated multi-stop journeys through Oman's most scenic regions. Follow the GPS, stop where you want, go at your own pace.

Bingo Challenges

Weekly photo challenges that turn exploration into a game. Visit real places, snap proof, earn XP. Camera-verified — no shortcuts.

Community Feed

Share discoveries, read reviews, trade tips with fellow explorers. See what's trending in your governorate right now.

Unique Stays

Book heritage properties, desert camps, and mountain lodges. Stay where the stories are — not in a chain hotel.

Volunteering

Join conservation and community projects across Oman. Give back to the places that give you so much.

Gamification
Explore. Photograph. Level up.
Every visit earns XP. Every photo completes a challenge. Watch your explorer rank climb from Fresh Visa to Honorary Omani.
